  A Few Important Things

Start the course by working your way through the Overview section, this details how the course works, how you can find your way around and where to go for help. It also includes important details about the assessments.

We also recommend using Astutis TrackSmart to help you structure your study, you'll find in the Astutis Support section.

You have 6 months from today to complete the course, including the assessments. If you do not complete the course in this time and require an extension, you will need to contact us for guidance.

  Assessments

Once you have completed the learning content, you will initially have 2 weeks from the day you complete the learning materials section (or until the expiry date of your course, if this comes first) to complete both course assessments.

If you do not complete them within the 2 weeks (or before the date of course expiry, if this comes first) this will be marked as a 'fail'. You will however be given 1 more opportunity to resubmit, once you have contacted us.

Once you have completed both assessments they are sent to IOSH to be verified. It is not until we have received verification of your marks from IOSH that we can confirm whether you have passed, or not.
