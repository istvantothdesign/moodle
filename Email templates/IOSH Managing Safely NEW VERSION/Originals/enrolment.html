Welcome to your Astutis IOSH Managing Safely online learning course. You will already have received an email with your login details to the Astutis online learning website but this email is intended to highlight a few important things about your course.

Start the course by working your way through the Overview and Support section, this details how the course works, how you can find your way around and where to go for help. It also includes important details about the assessments. 

You have 6 months from today to complete the course, including the assessments. If you do not complete the course in this time and require an extension, you will need to contact us for guidance.

Once you have completed the learning content, you will initially have 2 weeks from the day you complete the learning materials section (or until the expiry date of your course, if this comes first) to complete both course assessments. If you do not complete them within the 2 weeks (or before the date of course expiry, if this comes first) this will be marked as a 'fail'.  You will however be given 1 more opportunity to resubmit, once you have contacted us.

Once you have completed both assessments they are sent to IOSH to be verified. It is not until we have received verification of your marks from IOSH that we can confirm whether you have passed, or not.

If you have any questions please don't hesitate to contact us.
